## Nominative Sentence (starts with noun)

| عَرَبِيّ | English | Transliteration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| الْقَلَمُ مَكْسُورٌ | The Pen is Broken | _Al-Qalamu Maksuurun_ |
| الْبَابُ مَفْتُوحٌ | The Door is Open | _Al-Babu Maftuuhun_ |
| الْوَلَدُ جَالِسٌ وَ ملْمُدَرِّسُ وَاقِفٌ | The Boy is Sitting And The Teacher is Standing | _Al-Waladu Jaalisun Wa Al-mudarrisu waaqifun_ |
| الْكِتَابُ جَدِيدٌ وَ الْقَلَمُ قَدِيمٌ | The Book is New And The Pen is Old | _Al-Kitaabu Jadiidun Wa Al-Qalamu Qadiimun_ |
| الْحِمَارُ صَغِيرٌ وَ الْحِصَانُ كَبِيرٌ | The Donkey is Small And The Horse is Big | _Al-Himaaru Saghiirun Wa Al-Hisaanu Kabiirun_ |
| الْكُرْسِيُّ مَكْسُورٌ | The Chair is Broken | _Al-Kursiyyu Maksuurun_ |
| الْمَاءُ بَارِدٌ | The Water is Cold | _Al-Maa'u Baaridun_ |
| الْقَمَرُ جَمِيلٌ | The Moon is Beautiful | _Al-Qamaru Jamiilun_ |
| الْبَيْتُ قَرِيبٌ وَ الْمَسْجِدُ بَعِيدٌ | The House is Near And The Masjid is Far | _Al-Baytu Qariibun Wa Al-Masjidu Baeidun_ |
| الْحَجَرُ ثَقِيلٌ وَ الْوَرَقُ خَفِيفٌ | The Stone is Heavy And The Paper is Light | _Al-Hajaru Thaqiilun Wa Al-Waraqu Khafiifun_ |
| اللَّبَنُ حَارٌّ | The Milk is Hot | _Al-Labanu Haarrun_ |
| الْقَمِيصُ نَظِيفٌ | The Shirt is Clean | _Al-Qamiisu Naziifun_ |
